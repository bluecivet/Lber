How to register

To access Lber service, you have to register an account first. Depends on which account you register in, we have account just for driver and costomer. Of cause, you have have two. Here is example to register a costomer account. Registering driver account is same process.

First, click the sign in button at the right top of the page. Then choose a user type.

Finally, fill out form then you are all done!

How to log in

To access Lber service, you have to log in to panel page. Depends on which account you log in, we have account just for driver and costomer. Here is example to log in a costomer account. Loging in driver account is same process.

First, click the log in button at the right top of the page. Then choose a user type.

Finally, enter user name and password then you are all done!
